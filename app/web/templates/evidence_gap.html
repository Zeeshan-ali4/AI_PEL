The evidence gap AI PEL closes

Before running a scenario, this page makes the assurance problem explicit: without a deterministic policy enforcement layer, evidence is often reconstructed after the action. With AI PEL, evidence is captured at the gate, before execution, using fields and pages already visible in this demo.

Without / with contrast

What Risk can prove at decision time

Each paired point links the assurance concern to concrete demo surfaces or record fields.

Without a policy enforcement layer

Post-hoc-only visibility

You usually know what happened only after the agent has acted, by reading tool logs or transcripts.

With AI PEL

The scenario runner shows actions intercepted before execution and evaluated through the gate.

Without a policy enforcement layer

Implicit decision semantics

What counts as a decision is buried in free text, agent state, or application-specific status codes.

With AI PEL

Decision records expose standard fields such as decision.decision, decision.reason, control_id, and threshold_used.

Without a policy enforcement layer

Context reconstructed later

Rebuilding the facts means querying several systems later and hoping account status, approvals, and recipient data have not changed.

With AI PEL

Every evaluation stores the context used at decision time alongside bounded evidence; payment paths deliberately skip semantics where it is not needed.

Without a policy enforcement layer

No chain of custody

Logs may exist, but they do not prove whether a historical record has been changed since the action was evaluated.

With AI PEL

The audit log records record_hash and prev_hash, then exposes audit-chain verification and a labelled tamper demonstration.

Without a policy enforcement layer

Control traceability is unclear

A reviewer must infer which policy or framework obligation applied to the action.

With AI PEL

Decision views and records show triggered_controls and framework_mappings for the control that fired.

Without a policy enforcement layer

Human oversight evidence is thin

Approval can be separated from the original action, making accountability hard to evidence.

With AI PEL

The approvals queue routes escalations to a named role, and approval decisions append linked approval_decision records rather than mutating the original evaluation.
